About

Cambridge University Biological Society is dedicated to inviting the finest speakers and organising events for our community. Life membership can be purchased for Â£15 at any BioSoc event for access to speaker formals, talks recordings, and members-only events.


Talks

We host talks every other Monday in Michaelmas and Lent, and 1-2 in Easter, on various topics in biology, with speakers coming in from across the world. This year we are also hosting some panels in collaboration with other societies!

Socials

We host regular social events, like game sessions and formals, in collaboration, or with our speakers!

Others

This year, we will also be collaborating with Oxford and some London unis to reinstate Varsity Life Sciences, a poster sharing session for undergraduates. Stay tuned!
